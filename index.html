Delete your account – MyApp

If you’d like to delete your account and all associated data from MyApp:

  • Email us at delete@yourdomain.com with your account email address.
  • Our team will verify your request.
  • Once verified, we will permanently delete your account and data within 7 days.

Data that will be deleted

  • Email address
  • Profile data
  • All usage history

Data we may retain

  • Aggregated, non-personal data (for analytics)
  • Transaction records (if required by law) for up to 90 days

If you have questions, contact us at support@yourdomain.com.
